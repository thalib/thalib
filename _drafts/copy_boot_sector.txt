

Here I will explain how we will be backup and restore the boot sector of a running pc.

In  this exampe I will consider my hardisk is /dev/hda.

backing up existing bootsector in /dev/hda hardrive

dd if=/dev/hda of=bootsec.bin bs=512 count=1


Now to restore the bootsectore to the hardisk use the following commad.

dd if=bootsec.bin of=/dev/hda bs=512 count=1
